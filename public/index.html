about us

Meraki Structure is a structural consultant that works with clients to develop superior, practical, safe, efficient and economical designs for all stages of building constructions. We have a detailed understanding of the requirements needed for the clients and we provide services from initial concept and feasibility stages to complete design development. We mainly focus on to Create Structural designs and detailed execution drawings. In house civil engineering division to design the structure and work in tandem with the architects to provide appropriate solutions based on the design of the building. We have experience in working developing pre-engineered buildings, Precast buildings, R.C.C buildings, long span pretension systems.
